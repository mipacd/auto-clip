Overview

This site shows clips from Hololive automatically generated by chat log analysis, based on 30 second groupings of chat messages (lol, kusa, etc.). All clips are set 30 seconds prior to the largest grouping to provide context.

Instructions

Select a Sunday from the week dropdown to select a week (weeks are Sun-Sat JST). Then, select a streamer and type of clip.

Missing Streams

Not all streams are available in this tool. Some reasons a stream could be missing include: no chat replay, chat replay was removed due to video editing, chat replay wasn't available at the time of scan, or the chat replay is malformed on Youtube's end. Streams that were available at the time of the scan but are no longer available will still appear but will be unplayable.
